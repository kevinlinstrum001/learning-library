Learning Library · Curriculum Map

From first principles to Maxwell’s Equations.

A structured, expandable program in mathematics, physics, and engineering. Every lesson establishes one durable idea, then remains open for deeper explanations, practice, diagrams, and future connections.

Ultimate goal

Develop the knowledge required to understand Maxwell’s Equations and advanced technical subjects from first principles.

Purpose

Learn in a deliberate order.

Later topics build on earlier ones. The sequence begins with mathematical language, applies it to motion and forces, and then uses both to study electric and magnetic fields.

Expandable design

Begin clearly. Expand permanently.

Each HTML lesson starts with a strong core explanation and a compact worked example. Learning-prompt cards allow the lesson to grow without replacing its existing material.
